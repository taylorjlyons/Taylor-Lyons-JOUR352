Homers on the rise

Maryland baseball's home run totals have steadily increased in recent years

By Taylor Lyons

Maryland baseball set a program record for home runs in a season in 2022, slugging 137 long balls. Home run totals rose steadily in recent years leading up to the record as homers have become a staple in the offenses of both professional and college teams. Through just over half of the 2023 season, the Terps are on pace to come close to their mark from the prior season.
